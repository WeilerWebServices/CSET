Mosaic.

Fonts everywhere you need them.

An all-in-one solution for font discovery, expertise, collaboration and management. With Mosaic, you can free your creative teams to discover, share and prototype limitlessly with fonts – while making it easier to license, deploy, and manage usage of all your fonts across enterprise.

Get started

Ready to get started with Mosaic? Fill out our form and a sales representative will get back to you.

Get Started

Discover fonts.

Font discovery and unlimited prototyping.

Eliminate wasted creative spend and unnecessary design cycles. Get the right font for the job, every time.

 

Mosaic provides access to Monotype’s library of 14,000 fonts. Prototyping with all weights and character sets gives your team the confidence and freedom to do what they do best without having to worry about licensing or managing type across the organization. Font collections provide inspiration, while enhanced search allows fonts to be filtered by mood, style or name.

Enable fonts.

Enable fonts everywhere.

Remove one-off requests and bottlenecks by granting font access and rights to everyone who needs it – on an individual or team basis.

 

A unified approval process allows teams and individuals to immediately begin working with any font in their favorite creative app. With fonts that work no matter where they appear, global teams and external partners are able to deliver the high-quality experiences customers expect.

Manage fonts.

Monotype and Third-party font management.

Streamline the tools people use to discover, deploy and manage all your fonts – within a single application.

 

Large organisations now have a simple way to organise and track font distribution and usage among divisions and subsidiary companies. In addition to the Monotype library, Mosaic allows IT and Procurement teams to upload any third-party foundry’s fonts, centralizing licensing and font management in a single cloud-based service. New icons enable creatives to easily identify foundry source and associated rights – helping keep everyone in compliance.

Expertise.

Which Mosaic is right for you?

Three distinct options to help agencies and brands.

Whether you simply want to prototype freely with new fonts until you find the perfect fit; manage the distribution of fonts across your brand with confidence; or access the full breadth of Monotype’s expertise through Studio consultation and hands-on customer support, there’s a version of Mosaic for you.

Latest updates.

  • Bring any of your third-party fonts into Mosaic - manage your entire font library within a single application. 
  • Immediately activate and access complete font families in your favorite creative applications.
  • Gain visibility into font downloads and distribution across the organization with the in-product dashboard.
  • Standard support for all Mosaic customers including email and chat support, as well as digital onboarding.
